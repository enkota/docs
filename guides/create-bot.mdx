## Your first AI sales agent

1. Navigate to the [bots](https://supmaya.com/bots) page on the dashboard.
2. Click `Create bot` and add a name.
3. Follow the setup guide to connect your calendar, inbound/outbound channels and prompt scripts.
4. The bot will not be enabled by default, please ensure everything is setup first before testing your first lead flow.
