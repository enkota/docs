## Integrate with WhatsApp

1. Navigate to the `Bot` edit page and click the `Outbound channels` tab.
2. Click the `Connect` button under WhatsApp and click `Connect with Facebook`.
3. Login to your `Facebook` account that is related to your `Whatsapp Business Account`.
4. Follow the WhatsApp Business onboarding steps.

<Note>A seperate phone number is no longer required and you can pick `Use a display name only` during onboarding.</Note>

<img src="/images/bot-whatsapp-username.png" style={{width: "500px"}} />

### Add Meta payment method

5. Ensure a payment method is added before starting the bot. Meta charge for non-customer initiated messages and more information can be found [here](https://business.whatsapp.com/products/platform-pricing).

<img src="/images/bot-whatsapp-payment.png" style={{width: "400px"}} />

6. Once connected, navigate back to Maya and click `Manage settings` under `Outbound channels`.

### Set message template
<img src="/images/bot-whatsapp-template.png" style={{width: "400px"}} />

7. Pick your WhatsApp business template. If you haven't created any yet, please do so within the [Meta WhatsApp Manager](https://business.facebook.com/latest/whatsapp_manager/phone_numbers).

<Note>The Meta `message_template` must be approved before you are able to send any messages to leads.</Note>


### Register WhatsApp number

<img src="/images/bot-whatsapp-number.png" />

8. Ensure your WhatsApp username and free number is approved.

9. Once approved, head back to Maya and click `Manage settings` under the WhatsApp integration. Click `Register Number` to finish up integration.

10. Ensure the `Outbound channel` is set to WhatsApp under the `Outbound channels` on our dashboard and test things out on the `Playground`.
