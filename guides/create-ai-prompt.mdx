## AI qualification script

The goal of Supmaya is to convert and summarise higher quality leads to book on your external system or follow up via email/phone. Part of this flow is to automate the conversation using AI.

### Example script

### Questions

```
- What is the name of your company?
- What issues do you have with lead qualification at the moment?
- Do you have a CRM at the moment and would you like to integrate with it?
- Roughly how many lead opportunities do you expect per month?
```

### Knowledge Base

```
- Supmaya is an AI-powered sales assistant that helps businesses qualify leads and get them to follow up.
- We integrate with Zapier to send qualified leads directly to your CRM or email marketing platform.
- Plans start at $39/month for up to 50 opportunity conversations per month. $199/month for 250 opportunities, $499/month for 750 opportunities and custom plans on request.
```
