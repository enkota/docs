## AI qualification script

The goal of Maya is to convert higher quality leads to book within Calendly or your booking system. As part of this flow is to automate the conversation using AI. The `Prompt script` is the text that is used to give the bot context about your business.

1. Navigate to your bot edit page and click the `AI prompt` tab.

### Intro message
2. Enter your `Intro message` which is the first message that is sent to a lead. (Note: WhatsApp uses their own template system for that channel).

### Prompt script
You are welcome to write your own script, but we have included a handy AI prompt generator to get you started.

1. On the `AI prompt` tab, click `Create prompt with ai` button and fill out the fields.
2. Be consise yet informative. The goal is to guide the bot by qualifying, finding booking availability and provide the lead a booking link.
